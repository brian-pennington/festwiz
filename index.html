      Welcome to FestWiz

      Rating Artists

      Use the 1–4 buttons on each artist card to rate acts you're interested in seeing.

      • 1 — Skip
      • 2 — Maybe
      • 3 — Interested
      • 4 — Must-see

      Tap an artist's name to open the detail view, where you can add notes and access streaming links.

      Genre & Subgenre Tiers

      Use Genre Tiers and Subgenre Tiers to prioritize your listening queue. Set a genre to High to review those artists first, or Skip to hide them from view entirely.

      Schedule

      Switch to the Schedule page to see all shows on a grid organized by venue and time slot. Tap any show to see details and update your rating. Filter by rated status or admission type (Free, Paid, Badge).

      Sharing Your Ratings

      Hit Share to generate a URL containing your ratings. Send it to a friend — when they open the link they'll be prompted to import your ratings and can see how your picks compare.

      Exporting

      The Export button gives you two options:

      • Shortlist (CSV) — your 3+ rated artists with links, easy to share with anyone
      • Full Backup (JSON) — all ratings, notes, and tiers for safekeeping or moving to a new device

      Adding Shows

      On the Schedule page, use + Shows to manually add unofficial showcases or day parties not in the official lineup.
